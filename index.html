About GADA

Our Mission

To transform Algeria’s agricultural sector through technical engineering, investment structuring, and digital monitoring – always aligned with national regulations and the Ministry of Agriculture’s strategic priorities.

Our Vision

A modern, resilient agricultural economy that ensures food security, optimises natural resources, and positions Algeria as a regional leader in sustainable farming.

Core Objectives

  • Regulatory alignment with national agricultural policy
  • Engineering expertise for large‑scale projects
  • Digital integration (livestock satellite tracking, data analytics)
  • Investment structuring for domestic and international partners
